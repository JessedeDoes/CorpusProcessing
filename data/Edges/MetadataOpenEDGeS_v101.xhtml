The EDGeS Diachronic Bible Corpus – Metadata

Version 1.01

28 May 2021

This document provides full names and sources for the Bible translations included in the EDGeS Diachronic  Bible Corpus (short, EDGeS Corpus). The EDGeS corpus is available in two ways:

1. The complete collection is available for querying through the OPUS project ( https://opus.nlpl.eu ). Because  this contains material under copyright, access to the corpus search facilities is password protected.  

2. A subset of the material, called OpenEDGeS, has been made available for download under a public license  (CC BY-NC-SA 4.0). OpenEDGeS is available through Språkbankens resource infrastructure  ( https://spraakbanken.gu.se/en/resources/openedges ).

This information is listed to give credit where it is due, and to help users of the EDGeS Corpus to assess the  quality and other properties of an included translation by tracing its publication lineage. The heading of each  entry refers to the abbreviated Bible names in OpenEDGeS (the shorthand used in OPUS is given in between  brackets).

Before inclusion in the EDGeS Corpus, all source material has undergone considerable format conversion.  Typically, the sources contain much more information in the form of mark-up than has been included in the  EDGeS Corpus. In several cases other edits to the material were made; in particular versification may have  been added or changed.

Mere reference to the source is not to be taken as a claim that the source projects endorsed the EDGeS Corpus  or the use of their material in the EDGeS Corpus in any way.

EDGeS Diachronic Bible Corpus – Metadata OpenEDGeS

22 June 2020

This document provides full names and sources for the Bible translations included in OpenEDGeS.

This information is listed to give credit where it is due, and to help users of OpenEDGeS to assess the quality  and other properties of an included translation by tracing its publication lineage.

Before inclusion in OpenEDGeS, all source material has undergone considerable format conversion. Typically,  the sources contain much more information in the form of mark-up than has been included in OpenEDGeS. In  several cases other edits to the material were made; in particular versification may have been added or  changed.

Mere reference to the source is not to be taken as a claim that the source projects endorsed OpenEDGeS or  the use of their material in OpenEDGeS in any way.
